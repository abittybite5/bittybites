Yemista

With a side of Lemony Potatoes

Ingredients:

For the meatballs:
  • 250g or beef mince (or any mince of your choice)
  • 2cups medium grain rice
  • 4 capsicums
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 brown onion
  • 2Tbs of tomato paste
  • Handful of fresh parsley
  • Tinned diced tomatoes
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 1tsp of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 1.5cups of water
  • Olive oil
For the Potatoes:
  • Small bag of potatoes
  • 3 lemons - juiced
  • Olive oil
  • 4tsp of salt
  • 2tsp of black pepper

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C
  2. Dice a whole brown onion, pour olive oil into a large hot pan and toss onion pieces - turning the heat to low and leaving them for about 30 secs.
  3. Toss in a pack of mince and stir in the pan.
  4. Whilst the onion and mince cook, wash your medium grain rice.
  5. Cut off tops of tomatoes scooping out flesh, setting the insides aside for the sauce and the tops for cooking in the oven. (If the insides are too tough to break apart, chop finely.)
  6. Cut off the tops of the capsicum and take out the insides, setting the tops aside for cooking in the oven.
  7. Add tomato paste to the browned mince in the frying pan.
  8. Roughly chop fresh parsley. Add the insides of the tomato into the pan, along with the chopped parsley, salt, pepper and sugar.
  9. Add the water into the pan and simmer for 3 mins.
  10. Fill capsicums and tomatoes 3/4 of the way with the rice and mince and covering them again with the tops, drizzling the tops with olive oil.
  11. Peel potatoes and cut into wedges, placing them in a tray.
  12. Juice 3 lemons and set aside.
  13. Drizzle generous amount olive oil and lemon juice on the potatoes and evenly sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  14. Cover pans with aluminium foil and place in oven at 180°C fan forced and cook for 75 mins (until capsicum is tender).
  15. Once finished cooking, plate, and serve or store for later.
