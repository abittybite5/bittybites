Lemon Myrtle Cheesecake

Vegetarian

Ingredients:

For the base:
  • 250g Granita biscuits - crushed
  • 130g butter
For the filling:
  • 500g cream cheese
  • 3Tbs of lemon juice
  • 1.5cups of caster sugar
  • 2Tbs butter (melted)
  • 1Tbs of vanilla extract
  • 2Tbs Lemon Myrtle dried & milled
  • 600mL whipping cream
  • 3 sachets of Jel It In (Queen brand vegetarian gelatin)

Method:

Base:
  1. Process granita biscuits until fine.
  2. Melt butter in microwave - blitzing for 10 seconds until liquified.
  3. Combine granita biscuits and butter and press mixture into the base of a lined 8 inch spring form cake pan with the bottom of a glass or a potato masher.
  4. Place in fridge to set.
Filling:
  1. Put cream cheese, lemon juice and vanilla in food processor or nutri bullet.
  2. Mix on pulse/extract a few times. Wipe the insides a few times as you go.
  3. Add in sugar and mix on medium to high (the sugar will make a smother consistency).
  4. Add in lemon myrtle and mix again, wiping down inside with spoon to make sure it is all incorporated.
  5. Add in 200mL of cream and mix thoroughly until combined.
  6. Into a non-stick saucepan, place 400mL cream and 3 sachets of ‘Jel It In’.
  7. Mix with a spoon at first - then change to a whisk and make sure there are no lumps before placing on the heat.
  8. Place on a large cooktop element on low, and WHISK CONSTANTLY.
  9. Use a thermometer to check temperature. Once it reaches 77°C it should start bubbling.
  10. Take off heat whilst still whisking constantly - keep whisking off the heat for a further minute to allow to cool slightly. (Will be thick, but should be smooth).
  11. Pour into processor containing the cream cheese mixture and mix on high until smooth.
  12. Pour quickly onto biscuit base and place in fridge to chill for 4 hours, once set it is ready to serve.
