About Me

Hi there, I'm Aaliyah, the creator of Bitty Bites! Nice to meet you!

My recipes focus on my Greek and Aboriginal heritage. On my maternal side I am Greek, my Yiayia coming from central Greece and my Papou coming from the North. On my paternal side I am Aboriginal, my family comes from Wilman country in the Noongar region.

My Greek Heritage...

All my life I have known about my Greek heritage. I have fond memories of helping my yiayia and great yiayia braid and twist the koulourakia for Easter. When I was younger I would jump around on a chair, (used to reach the kitchen bench), and my great yiayia would shout “tha peseis! tha peseis!”. I thought it was funny at the time, turns out that she was worried, telling me “you are going to fall!”. My great yiayia was born in Greece and lived in a village called Aliveri before moving to Australia. My yiayia was born here in Australia.

My Noongar Heritage...

It a bit was later in my early years that I came to understand that I was Aboriginal. I never really paid atten- tion or asked about why I didn’t have a grandma on my paternal side, and why my dad had olive skin despite my grandad having pale skin. I found out that my grandma and grandad di- vorced whilst my dad was still young, and my grandma wasn’t really present in his life after this. This caused my connection to his Noongar heritage to be broken and lost. I never got the chance to meet my grandma, she had sadly passed away just before we had made plans meet her. When she was younger she was sent to the Roelands Mission after her mum passed away and lived there until she finished school.
