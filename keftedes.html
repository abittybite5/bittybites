Keftedes "Greek Meatballs"

With a side of Tzatziki

Ingredients:

For the meatballs:
  • 500g or beef mince (or any mince of your choice)
  • 1 medium red onion grated
  • 5 slices of white bread soaked in water (excess water removed)
  • 1tsp of minced garlic
  • 1 egg
  • 2Tbs of chopped fresh parsley
  • 2Tbs of chopped fresh mint leaves
  • Olive oil for cooking
For the Tzatziki:
  • 1/4 continental cucumber
  • 1.5cups of Greek yogurt
  • 1tsp of salt
  • 1Tbs of minced garlic
  • Drizzle of olive oil (optional)

Method:

  1. In a bowl, mix mince, onion, soaked bread, garlic, parsley, mint and egg.
  2. Create meatballs, should be rolled to be the size of a quarter of your palm.
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan.
  4. Place as many meatballs as possible in the frying pan, turn ing them over until browned and evenly cooked through.
  5. Set aside meatballs in a bowl, gathering tzatziki ingredients.
  6. Chop cucumber into small cubes (brunoise) pieces, or grate.
  7. Mix Greek yogurt, chopped cu- cumber, minced garlic and salt in a small bowl.
  8. All done and ready to serve with some pita bread.
