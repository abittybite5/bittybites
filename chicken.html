Bush Spiced Roast Chicken

With a side of Roast Vegetables

Ingredients:

For the chicken:
  • Whole chicken
  • Herbie’s bbq bush spice mix 30g
  • 3Tbs minced garlic
  • 4Tbs olive oil
  • 2Tbs butter (melted)
  • 2tsp salt
  • 1 lemon, cut into quarters
  • 1/2 a lemon, juiced
  • 2 garlic cloves
For the vegetables:
  • Dried thyme
  • 1x lemon, juiced
  • 3 large potatoes
  • Bunch of baby carrots

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. De-gut chicken, if need be, and place in a baking tray.
  3. To make the spice mix, combine melt- ed butter, olive oil, salt, lemon juice, garlic and Herbie’s spice mix into a bowl.
  4. Poke small holes into the chicken and rub the spice mix all over it - making sure to also get underneath the skin.
  5. Stuff chicken with garlic and a sliced lemon to keep moist.
  6. Sprinkle a pinch of salt on the chicken and cover tray with alfoil.
  7. Place covered chicken into the oven for 70 minutes.
  8. For roast vegetables, cut potatoes into quarters and peel carrots - placing them in a tray.
  9. Drizzle them in olive oil and lemon juice, and sprinkle with salt and thyme.
  10. Cover the veggies and roast in the oven for 60 minutes.
  11. Uncover the chicken and veggies to brown for about 20 minutes.
  12. To check if chicken is cooked, insert a thermometer to make sure it reads over 75°C.
  13. Chicken and veggies are now ready to serve!
