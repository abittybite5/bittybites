Aboriginal Culture...

Aboriginal people made use of the land, managing it, producing agriculture and utilising fire to encourage plant growth. We were not hunter gatherers.

Many of our native herbs, fruits and fauna are used today in modern cooking for their unique flavours and health benefits, such as native finger-lime on oysters, kangaroo rissoles or lemon myrtle tea. Our herbs often also have medicinal properties, for instance the Australian peppermint tree was used as an equivalnet to Vicks VapoRub.
